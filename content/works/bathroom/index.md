他覺得臉上有點癢，但伸手去抓時，癢的感覺卻已經消失了，他的手就這 樣懸在空中，懸在抓與不抓之間，懸在兩面山壁之間。 「他們去影印好久喔。」 不知何時身邊多出一道人影，拿著手機，螢幕的光源投射出一面短暫而與周遭不同的 世界，他望向山頭的另一端，兩人的視線再空中錯開。在這一刻，他背後的地面發出 一陣金屬與硬物碰撞的聲響，一張反射著夜色的金屬片掉落在地上。


His face felt slightly itchy, but when he raised his hand to scratch it, the sensation had already faded. His hand remained suspended in the air—hovering between scratching and not scratching, between two opposing cliff faces.

“They’ve been photocopying for a long time.”

At some point, a figure had appeared beside him, holding a phone. The glow of the screen cast a brief world of light, distinct from the surrounding darkness. He looked toward the other side of the mountain ridge, and their gazes crossed in midair without meeting. At that moment, a sharp sound of metal striking something hard rang out behind him, and a sheet of metal reflecting the night fell to the ground.
